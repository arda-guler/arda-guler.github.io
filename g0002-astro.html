Comet NEOWISE back when it was somewhat close to Sol. I waited a long time to be able to observe it when it was the brightest, but had little luck.
I then thought I could capture it with my telescope as it was leaving the vicinity of Sol, so with hope, I looked at where it was supposed to be in the sky.

I compared my photos and overlaid it onto sky maps at that day, and I believe that that dot may be NEOWISE.
